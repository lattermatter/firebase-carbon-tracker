How It Works

Welcome to our Carbon Tracker application! This web app helps you monitor and reduce your carbon footprint through user-friendly tools and personalized recommendations.

Key Features:

  • Track Your Carbon Footprint: Input your daily activities, such as energy usage and transportation, to gain insights into your carbon emissions.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Receive tailored tips and suggestions based on your input to help you reduce your carbon impact.
  • Visualization Tools: Visualize your data with graphs and charts to see your progress over time.
  • Gamification: Stay motivated by achieving goals and earning rewards for reducing your carbon footprint.

How to Use the App

  1. Create an Account: Sign up to create your profile and start tracking your carbon emissions.
  2. Input Your Data: Enter your daily energy consumption, transportation methods, and dietary choices.
  3. Get Insights: Review your carbon footprint and receive personalized recommendations for reducing emissions.
  4. Monitor Progress: Use our visual tools to track your improvements over time.
  5. Engage and Earn: Participate in challenges and earn a badge for reaching your goals!

Technology Stack

This application is built using the following technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Backend: Firebase for Authentication, Firestore for data storage
  • Data Visualization: Google cloud functions with Python
  • AI Features: Implemented graph predictions using Python with scikitlearn, uses nltk for NLP
  • Hosting: Deployed on Firebase Hosting for seamless performance and scalability
